Privacy

This is a static portfolio website. It’s designed to be lightweight and respectful of privacy.

Data collection

  • The “Contact” form opens your email client; messages are not stored by this site.
  • No advertising trackers are intentionally included.

Third-party links

This site links to third-party services (e.g., GitHub, LinkedIn, Credly, program pages). Those sites may collect data under their own policies.
